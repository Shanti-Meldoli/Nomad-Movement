About me

Hi, I am Shanti, the person behind Nomad Movement. I'd love to share the journey that led me here and the vision that drives our mission.

It all began from a place many find familiar - a sedentary lifestyle that left me feeling disconnected from my own body and potential. At 18, I decided it was time for a change, not just in how I looked, but in how I felt and lived my life.

Not sure on how to begin, I started doing home workouts from YouTube, not knowing what I was doing and simply following the exercises. After some weeks I started seeing not only my body changing but I was feeling happier by being a more active person.

It was during this time that my interest in fitness grew, and I started researching the science behind exercises and programming. This led me to invest time and money in obtaining a certification in High-Performance Functional Training from the ISAF Institute.

Over the years, I've experimented with various types of training, from home routines to hypertrophy and powerlifting programs in the gym. I've even explored calisthenics for nearly two years without setting foot in a gym.
My current approach is a hybrid style where I don't limit myself to a single discipline, and there's always a purpose behind my workouts, whether it's learning a new movement, increasing strength in a specific exercise, improving my mobility or strengthening a part of the body that needs attention.

Nomad Movement is the embodiment of these experiences, striving to make fitness accessible, personalized, and deeply transformative. We aim to break the chains of a sedentary lifestyle, advocating for a world where movement is not just routine but a foundational aspect of daily life.

The greatest lesson learned, both personally and through coaching, is the undeniable power of consistency. As your trainer, my mission is to tailor your exercise routine to fit seamlessly into your life, fostering sustainable change.
